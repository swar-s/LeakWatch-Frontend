What to do

  • Change passwords immediately on affected services and any reused elsewhere.
  • Enable multi-factor authentication (MFA) on all accounts.
  • Monitor accounts for suspicious activity or unauthorized access.
  • Be cautious of phishing attempts and verify sender authenticity.

What not to do

  • Do not reuse passwords across multiple sites.
  • Do not ignore notifications about breaches affecting your accounts.
  • Do not click on suspicious links or download attachments from unknown sources.
  • Do not share passwords or sensitive information via email or messaging apps.
